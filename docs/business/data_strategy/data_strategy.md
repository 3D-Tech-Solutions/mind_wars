Mind Wars: Data Strategy Master Blueprint

1. Vision & Executive Summary

The Mind Wars data strategy is built on the principle of Informed Value Exchange. We provide a premium, health-focused social gaming experience for free to users in exchange for high-fidelity, anonymized cognitive telemetry. This data serves as a longitudinal biomarker for health, a screening tool for recruitment, and a research asset for neuroscience.

3. The Core Matrix: Data Tiers

Our strategy categorizes data into three layers of abstraction:

Layer A: Atomic Telemetry (The "Physical" Layer)

Hardware Sensors: Touch pressure, jitter, latency, screen refresh correlation.

Input Biometrics: Reaction time, ballistic precision, micro-tremors.

Hardware Metadata: Device model, OS, battery health (proxy for economic status).

Layer B: Cognitive Pillars (The "Mental" Layer)

Memory: Working span, interference resistance, decay curves.

Logic: Deductive depth, rule-acquisition speed, optimization delta.

Attention: Switch cost, vigilance decrement, inhibition control.

Spatial: Rotation velocity, path efficiency, perspective shifting.

Layer C: Social & Identity (The "Context" Layer)

Genealogy: Last-name clustering, hereditary performance deltas.

Social Graph: Influence scores, competitive peak vs. social choke.

Persona Enrichment: LinkedIn job title correlation, seniority-to-speed ratios.

4. Value Realization Strategies

4.1 Medical & Clinical (Longitudinal)

We utilize Digital Phenotyping to detect sub-clinical shifts in neuro-health.

Target: Pharma (Alzheimer's/Parkinson's research), Insurance (Risk assessment).

Strategy: Sell access to anonymized cohorts showing specific "Atrophy Indices."

4.2 Corporate & Recruitment (Snapshot)

We map game performance to professional success metrics.

Target: HR Platforms, Fortune 500 Recruiters.

Strategy: Provide a "Job Fit Score" based on cognitive archetypes (e.g., the "Strategic Architect" profile).

4.3 Educational & EdTech (Growth)

We track learning velocity to predict academic outcomes.

Target: Universities, K-12 Learning Platforms.

Strategy: Sell benchmarks for "Neuroplasticity" and "Attention Spans" across demographics.

5. Implementation Roadmap

Phase 1 (Ingestion): Deploy the game_events schema to capture Atomic Telemetry.

Phase 2 (Profiling): Run batch processing to generate Cognitive Radar Profiles.

Phase 3 (Enrichment): Integrate Social Graph (Last-name/LinkedIn) to add context.

Phase 4 (Monetization): Launch the "Insight Marketplace" for Tier 4 research partners.
